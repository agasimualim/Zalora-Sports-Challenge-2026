How to Join
1
Get your invite code
Contact the admin (panitia) to receive your team's invite code. Each team has a unique code.
2
Register
Click Register on the leaderboard page. Enter your invite code and name to join.
3
Submit your activities
Go to the Submit tab. Upload a screenshot of your workout from any fitness app (Strava, Garmin, Coros, Apple Health, Suunto, Huawei, etc). The AI will read the stats automatically.
4
Review & Confirm
Double-check the extracted data (calories, distance, duration, date). Fix any mistakes, then click Confirm. Your points will appear on the leaderboard.
Frequently Asked Questions
What fitness apps are supported?
Any app that shows calorie, distance, duration, and date information. This includes Strava, Garmin Connect, Coros, Apple Health, Suunto, Huawei Health, Nike Run Club, and more.

Tip: Take a clear screenshot showing the full activity summary for best results.
The AI read my stats wrong. What do I do?
After the AI analyzes your screenshot, you'll see a review form. You can edit any field (calories, distance, duration, date, activity name, sport type) before confirming. Double-check all numbers before submitting.
I don't have a screenshot. Can I still submit?
Screenshots help verify activities, but if you can't provide one, contact the admin via WhatsApp for manual entry.
"Invite code limit reached" — what now?
Each invite code has a limited number of uses. If yours is full, ask the admin (panitia) to generate a new code or reset the existing one.
I'm in the wrong team.
Your team is determined by the invite code you used. If there's an error, contact the admin to correct it.
How are points calculated?
Check the Scoring Guide tab on the leaderboard for a full breakdown. Points are based on calories, distance, duration, streaks, and daily calorie rankings.
What makes an activity valid?
Your activity must meet the minimum duration for its sport type (e.g. 20 min for Running, 30 min for Cycling). If calories are recorded, it must also have at least 4 kcal/min. Invalid activities don't earn points.
Can I delete an activity?
Activities cannot be deleted from the app. If you submitted wrong data, contact the admin (panitia) for correction.
